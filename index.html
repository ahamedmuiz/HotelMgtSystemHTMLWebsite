Discover your coastal escape at Weligama Bay Marriott Resort & Spa

Relax in luxury at our premier 5-star retreat
in Weligama, Sri Lanka.

Set along Sri Lanka’s radiant southern coastline, Weligama Bay Marriott Resort & Spa is a five-star beachfront sanctuary overlooking the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean.

With 198 elegantly appointed rooms and suites, each featuring plush bedding, marble bathrooms with oversized soaking tubs, spacious work areas, and private balconies offering uninterrupted ocean views, the resort blends luxury with coastal serenity.

Guests are invited to indulge in world-class culinary experiences across a variety of signature venues. From global flavors at Weligama Kitchen and fresh seafood at Big Fish, to handcrafted cocktails at Tides and Surf’s Up Bars, artisan treats at Mirissa Baking Company, and seasonal delights at the Pop-up Restaurant, every meal is a celebration of taste and ambiance.

Perfectly positioned for both relaxation and adventure, the resort offers easy access to iconic southern attractions. Explore the historic charm of Galle Fort, witness the traditional art of stilt fishing, embark on whale watching excursions in Mirissa, or ride the waves at the renowned Weligama Bay surf point.

After a day of discovery, unwind at Quan Spa, lounge by the infinity pool, stay active in the fully equipped fitness center, or enjoy romantic sunset strolls along the beach.

Weligama Bay Marriott is also a premier destination for weddings and events. With versatile indoor and outdoor venues, breathtaking coastal backdrops, and expert planning services, the resort transforms special moments into unforgettable celebrations.

Whether you're here to relax, explore, celebrate, or simply soak in the beauty of the southern coast, Weligama Bay Marriott Resort & Spa offers a refined escape where elegance meets the ocean.
